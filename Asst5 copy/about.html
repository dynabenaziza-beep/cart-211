Humans of New York

About the Project

Humans of New York began as a small photography project, with the goal of creating a visual map of the people who live in New York City. Over time, the portraits were paired with short interviews, turning the project into a large archive of personal stories.

The mission of Humans of New York is simple: to show that every person has a story worth hearing. By sharing these moments publicly, the project invites empathy, curiosity, and connection between strangers.

This website is a student project that recreates the idea in a very minimal way, focusing on clear text, simple images, and quiet space around each story.
