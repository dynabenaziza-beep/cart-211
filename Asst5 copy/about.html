Humans of New York

About the Project

Humans of New York began in 2010 as a photography project created by Brandon Stanton. The original goal was to photograph ten thousand New Yorkers and create a visual census of the city.

Over time, the project evolved from simple portraits to deeper stories—told through long-form captions and interviews that reveal the emotions, struggles, and experiences of everyday people. Today, Humans of New York has a global audience of more than twenty million followers.

The mission of the project is to capture honest, intimate glimpses into the lives of strangers, and to highlight the humanity and dignity in each of their stories.
