Humans of New York

About the Project

The Humans of New York project was started by Brandon Stanton in 2010. Originally, the goal was a simple photographic mission: to capture 10,000 portraits of New Yorkers and create a comprehensive visual catalog of the city's inhabitants.

Over time, the project evolved into a platform that paired powerful photographs with detailed, personal stories gathered through interviews. This combination transformed it into a beloved blog and social media sensation.

Today, HONY has a massive worldwide following, providing daily, intimate glimpses into the lives, struggles, and joys of everyday people on the streets of New York City and occasionally from around the globe.

You can find the original work and more stories on humansofnyarchive.com.
