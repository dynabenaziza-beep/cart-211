About Humans of New York

Humans of New York began in 2010 as a small photography project by Brandon Stanton. His initial mission was simple: photograph 10,000 New Yorkers and build a visual census of the city.

As the project grew, the portraits evolved into personal stories. People began sharing honest, emotional, and sometimes vulnerable moments from their lives. These captions transformed HONY into a unique form of storytelling where ordinary individuals became the center of powerful narratives.

Today, Humans of New York has millions of followers worldwide. The project continues to offer raw and intimate glimpses into the lives of strangers, reminding us that every person carries a story worth listening to.
