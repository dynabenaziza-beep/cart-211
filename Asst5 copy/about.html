Humans of New York

About the Project

Humans of New York is a photography and storytelling project started by Brandon Stanton in 2010. What began as a goal to photograph ten thousand New Yorkers has grown into a global archive of portraits and personal stories from people all over the world.

Each post combines an image with a short caption or longer narrative, offering a glimpse into the subject’s life. Together, these stories explore themes of love, loss, work, family, and identity, while emphasizing the dignity and complexity of every person.
