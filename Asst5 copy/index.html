Humans of New York

Humans of New York is a portrait-style storytelling project that began with one photographer walking the streets of New York City and asking strangers to share a small piece of their lives. Each story is short, personal, and rooted in everyday moments.

This student website is a simple tribute to that idea: a quiet space to browse faces, fragments, and feelings captured in the city.
