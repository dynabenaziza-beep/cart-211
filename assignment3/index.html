Ocean Vuong

Ocean Vuong, born in 1988 in Ho Chi Minh City (Vietnam), is a Vietnamese American poet, novelist, and professor. He emigrated to the United States with his family in 1990 and grew up in Connecticut. Author of the critically acclaimed poetry collections Night Sky with Exit Wounds and Time Is a Mother, as well as the New York Times bestselling novel On Earth We’re Briefly Gorgeous. A recipient of the MacArthur Fellowship and the American Book Award, he currently splits his time between Northampton, Massachusetts, and New York City.

Ocean Vuong’s work often explores themes of identity, memory, love, and loss,
blending personal history with
collective trauma. His writing combines poetry and narrative
to form an intimate yet universal voice.
