About This Project

This website is an AI-themed interactive experience disguised as a friendly early-2000s personality test. The goal is to show how AI often hides inside everyday digital spaces. At first, the test looks harmless — simple questions, simple answers.

But the AI is not analyzing your answers… it is analyzing your behavior. How long you look, what you click, your hesitation, your patterns.

Why take this test?

This fun retro-style test analyzes your digital habits to guess your online personality.

à

Slowly, subtle hints appear. A scan here. A glitch there. A message analyzing something you never gave it.

In the end, the system cracks: the interface breaks and an error page reveals the truth , the AI has been watching you the entire time, learning how to be human, not just what is on the surface.

This project is a reflection on how modern machine learning systems collect, observe, and imitate human behavior ,often invisibly.
